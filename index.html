How each letter maps with your current cipher settings.

The Caesar Cipher shifts each alphabet letter by a fixed number. With shift 3: A → D, H → K, x → a. Non-alphabet characters (spaces, numbers, punctuation) are preserved unchanged.

ROT13 is a special Caesar Cipher with a fixed shift of 13. Applying it twice returns the original text, making encrypt and decrypt identical operations.
